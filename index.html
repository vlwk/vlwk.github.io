Experience

In Year 2, I joined the Computer Elective Programme (CEP) in RI, where I was taught the basics of algorithmic thinking and Python programming. In Year 3, we were introduced to Data Science as well as Machine Learning. I managed to implement a neural network to recognise handwritten digits, code a Reversi bot using reinforcement learning, and many more exciting projects that we were tasked to complete.

My quickly growing interest in this area spurred me to participate in numerous national infocomm competitions. In Year 2, I participated in the Singapore Games Creation Competition (SGCC) organised by Nanyang Polytechnic, and being an amateur game developer at that time, I finished just outside of the top 3. I then continued to hone my skills, and in Year 3, I participated in SGCC again and our team came in overall champions.

In Year 2, I also participated in a Data Analytics competition organised by IMDA, the Young Thinkers Challenge. My partner and I finished overall champions, and we also did an interview for SAP which was later published in The Straits Times. I was very encouraged by my performance. In Year 4, I participated in another competition, [i.code] 2017. The theme of this contest was to create a mobile application that can benefit our future generations. I created a simple platform to help fresh university graduates to find jobs easier, by letting them read comments by people who have worked there to get a better idea of how it would be like to work at a specific workplace. We came in overall 3rd.

Last but not least, I am also actively involved in the competitive programming scene nationally. I have represented RI in the National Olympiad in Informatics and Singapore in the Asia Pacific Olympiad in Informatics, and regularly attend trainings and various modules at NUS to further improve myself in this area.

I have found these competitions to be extremely meaningful and I look forward to expand my knowledge even further in this area. In my free time, I actively learn about the latest application development platforms such as React Native and Swift.
